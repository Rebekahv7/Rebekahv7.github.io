Hardware Chart

Item Name Picture Purpose Price How delicate
Motherboard The main purpose is to store the CPU, memory and the connectors for input and output. The average motherboard costs 50$-200$ depending on what is needed/wanted. Normally around 100$. A motherboard is not typically delicate, but different parts on and within the Motherboard are more delicate than others.
Hard Disk Drive The purpose of a Hard Disk Drive is storing and retrieving digital data. The average price is from 50$- 150$ depending on what specifically is needed or wanted. Hard disk drives are very delicate and can be easily broken or damaged.
DVD Drive The purpose of a DVD Drive is to allow the system to play DVDs and other audio. The average price of a DVD drive is around 20$-100$ depending on what is wanted and needed specifically. DVD drives are fairly strong, but should be taken care of, like any other item.
Power Supply The purpose of a power supply is to provide power to the whole computer so it can display what the computer is running. The average price of a power supply is around 60$- 100$. Power supplies are noramlly durable but should not be droped, they might not be able to take the drop without damage.
RAM The purpose of RAM is to store and retrieve files from the computer. It is used to make files easier to access. The average price for RAM is around 60$ but the price can range from 40$- the low end of 100$. RAM is fairly durable, but should be handled carefully, just like any other hardware component.
